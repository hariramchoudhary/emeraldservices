WHAT IS MERCHANT NAVY


MERCHANT NAVY is intended to give you information on Merchant shipping and help you to choose your future career in ships a career which is professionally sound and satisfying, merchant shipping means trading in commercial commodities such as gernal cargo industrial goods oil tanks chemical tanker, liquid cargo, live stocks, gas etc. which are transported by sea going ships on international waters between various countries of the world.This is primarily a male oriented career, althought there is no legal bar for females taking up this profession.the career provides an opportunity for person to visit and acquaint with nationals of various countries and learn many things highly demand for indian seafarers in international market.
