Placements in merchant navy

Seafarers Shipping Services has retained its reputation as one of the best maritime training college in Delhi, India, instructing Pre-sea training for deck cadets and rating cadets.

To become a merchant navy officer, you need to undertake specialist training to accomplish professional seafaring certification and educational qualifications. This takes place at sea, on board shipping companies' vessels, and in specialist colleges and universities.

Seafarers Maritime Academy, has taken up the mission to provide world class academic as well as industry oriented curriculum to the students who aspire to serve the shipping industry. Our aim is to provide the best maritime training that helps students who are willing to join merchant navy and lead the nation towards prosperity and growth.
